Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 5, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how The Memory Pulse collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you visit our website or purchase our program.

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal identification data: name, email address, and other details you provide when completing a purchase.
  • Payment information: processed securely by Hotmart; we do not store credit card or banking data directly.
  • Usage data: browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on the site, collected via analytics tools.
  • Communications: messages you send us via email or WhatsApp support.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Process and deliver your purchase.

  • Provide customer support.

  • Send relevant communications (you may opt out at any time).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with trusted service providers necessary to operate our business (e.g., Hotmart for payment processing), and solely for that purpose.

Our website may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your browsing experience and collect analytics data. You can control cookie settings through your browser. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

5. Third-Party Platforms

This site may contain links to or embed content from third-party services (e.g., Facebook, Google, Hotmart). These parties have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. We are not responsible for their practices.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy or as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us through our support channels.

9. Children's Privacy

Our program is intended for adults only. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 18.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

11. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team via the email or WhatsApp provided in the members area.
